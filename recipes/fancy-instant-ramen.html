Fancy Instant Ramen

Description

Ever felt like instant ramen, yet it just felt a bit unspectacular?

Well, fear not! For this recipe will only take a few minutes of preparation, yet it will leave nothing to be desired.

Ingredients

  • Sesame Oil
  • Avocado Oil
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Ginger
  • Chicken Broth
  • Soy Sauce [Low Sodium if possible]
  • Siracha or hot chili garlic sauce
  • Shredded Carrots
  • Scallions
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Soft-boiled Egg

Steps

  1. PHeat the oil in a medium-sized saucepan
  2. Once the oil is hot, add garlic and ginger and simmer until fragant
  3. Add the rest of the veggies you want to add and cook until they soften up
  4. Transfer the broth to the pot, add soiy sauce and Siracha. Give it a good stir.
  5. Let it simmer for about 5 minutes, then give it a test and add more sauces accordingly
  6. Whilst the soup simmers, cook the Noodels in a seperate pot as per package instructions
  7. When done, rinse under cold water, place into the soup bowl and set it aside
  8. Once the broth is ready, spoon it over the noodles, garnish the bowl as you please
