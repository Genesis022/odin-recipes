Frenchtoast

Description

A Lazy Frenchtoast Recipe for Breakfast and Dinner alike!

Preparation will only take a few minutes and ingredients you already have at home, so whats holding you back?

Ingredients

  • 4 Slices of Toast
  • 2 Eggs
  • 150ml of milk
  • A few more

Steps

  1. Mix Eggs, Milk and [..] in a bowl
  2. Put a pan on medium heat, put the toast into the mix, then in the pan.
  3. Wait a minute, flip the toast, done.
